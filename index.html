LAKES OF ICELAND

01.12.24 - 11.12.24

You will visit Jokulsarlon, a glacial lake where you can watch drifting icebergs and climb to the top of the volcano.

During your adventure you will also visit Lake Myvatn, famous for its geothermal baths and geysers. Here you can relax and enjoy the beautiful thermal baths with panoramic views of the volcanoes and lake.
